Membership Benefits
NP Membership
Are you tired of not having superpowers? Our NP Membership level won't give you the ability to fly or turn invisible. You won't even get a pet rock or a plant that grows money. Upgrade to one of our higher levels and at least get something out of your membership!
Bronze Membership
Join our Bronze level and gain the power to control the weather! You'll also receive a free lifetime supply of unicorn tears and a personal assistant who moonlights as a ninja. Plus, you'll receive a pet dragon and your own personal paparazzi to capture your every moment.
Silver Membership
Upgrade to our Silver level and gain the ability to speak every language in the universe! You'll also receive a flying car, a personal chef who specializes in gourmet astronaut food, and a butler who can teleport.
Gold Membership
Our Gold membership is the ultimate level of access to all the Chamber of Commerce has to offer, including the power of telekinesis! You'll also receive a personal jetpack, a yacht that can travel through time, and a personal assistant who doubles as a footstool.
