About Us

Our Rich (Imaginary) History
Bountiful Foods was founded in 1850 by a group of eccentric foodies who believed in the power of smoothies to cure all ailments, from the common cold to a broken heart.
Legend has it that our founders, Dr. Smoothie and Lady Greens, traveled the world in search of the finest sustainably sourced foods and organic ingredients. Along the way, they discovered magical fruits and veggies that could make even the grumpiest unicorn smile.
Over the years, Bountiful Foods has grown from a small roadside stand to a bustling smoothie mecca in the heart of the Bay Area. Despite our humble beginnings, we've never lost sight of our mission: to spread joy, one delicious and nutritious sip at a time.
Our Awesome Bay Area
Located in the heart of the mystical Bay Area, Bountiful Foods is nestled among majestic redwoods and sparkling beaches. Our shop is surrounded by an enchanted forest, where fairies and gnomes frolic, and unicorns graze on kale.
The Bay Area is also home to a vibrant community of eco-conscious foodies who share our passion for sustainability and organic goodness. Our customers are like family to us, and we treat them with the same care and attention we give to our smoothies.
With its breathtaking natural beauty, innovative tech scene, and an abundance of yoga studios, the Bay Area is truly a magical place to live and work. Come visit us and experience the wonder for yourself!
