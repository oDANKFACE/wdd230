Target Audience

Description

Members of the chamber, individuals/organizations in support of the chamber's objectives, and individuals/ organizations interested in learning about the chamber's objectives.

Personas

Persona #1

Small Business Owner

Name: Karen Smith

Age: 45

Occupation: Owner of a retail store

Location: Cedar Pass Ranch

Education: Bachelor's degree in Business Administration

Karen is a successful small business owner who is actively involved in her community. She is a member of the local chamber of commerce and values the networking opportunities and resources it provides. Karen is busy running her business, but she makes time to stay informed about local events and initiatives that could impact her business. She uses the chamber of commerce website regularly to stay up-to-date on industry news, events, and member benefits. She is interested in resources that can help her improve and grow her business, such as business training workshops and mentorship programs.

Persona #2

Young Professional

Name: Jack Doe

Age: 27

Occupation: Marketing Manager

Location: Eagle Mountain City Center

Education: Master's degree in Marketing

Jack is a driven young professional who is eager to get involved in his community. He is a recent transplant to the area and is looking for ways to connect with other young professionals and make a difference in his community. Jack is tech-savvy and relies heavily on the internet for information and networking. He values the chamber of commerce as a source of information about local businesses and events. He uses the chamber's website to learn about networking opportunities, professional development events, and volunteer opportunities. Jack is interested in resources that can help him advance his career and make connections in the business community.

Scenarios

  1. Karen is visiting the site to find information about the next networking event. She wants to see the details of the event and register to attend.
  2. Jack is searching for local businesses that offer products and services that he needs. He wants to find information about the businesses, their products, and services and contact them if necessary.
  3. Karen wants to find resources that can help her improve her business skills. She visits the site to find information about business training workshops, mentorship programs, and other resources offered by the chamber.
  4. Jack wants to learn about volunteer opportunities in the community. He visits the site to find information about volunteer opportunities, the organizations that need volunteers, and how he can get involved.
  5. Karen wants to stay up-to-date on local events and initiatives that could impact her business. She visits the site to find information about local news, events, and initiatives, and to see how the chamber is involved in these initiatives.
